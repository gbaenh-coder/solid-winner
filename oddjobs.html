How It Works

Simple. Fast. Secure.

Whether you're looking for work or hiring — OddJob.ng makes the whole process painless.

01
📝

Post or Apply

Clients post jobs in minutes. Freelancers browse and apply with a single tap. No long forms, no gatekeeping.

02
🤝

Match & Connect

Our smart matching connects you with the right talent or client based on skill, location, and budget.

03
🔒

Secure Escrow

Payment is held safely in escrow until the job is done. No more stories of clients disappearing with work.

04
💸

Get Paid Instantly

Once approved, money hits your bank account or wallet within minutes. Naira, always.
